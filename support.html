Questions? Answers.

How do I change password?

You can change your password through your account or clicking the forget password link in the login page.

How to delete account?

You can delete your account by contacting a VeritasPay employee. This is necessary as we would like to know the reasons for removing your account.

How Do I use VeritasPay?

You can use VeritasPay to make online purchases with merchants or shopping platforms with VeritasPay options like Shopee and Lazada. For f2f transactions, only certain shops in Singapore may provide VeritasPay payment.
